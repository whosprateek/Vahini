# Vahini — Non‑technical Overview

Welcome to Vahini, a modern web application for monitoring and managing low‑tension (LT) power grids. This guide explains, in plain language, what the app does and how you can use it.

What Vahini does
- Real‑time view of the grid: See current system status and key indicators on a dashboard.
- Fault detection: Detects potential issues and alerts you promptly.
- Maintenance scheduling: Plan and track tasks for field technicians.
- Attendance and sessions: Keep track of who is signed in and their sessions.
- Notifications: Receive in‑app alerts about important events.

Who it’s for
- Grid operators and managers monitoring day‑to‑day operations
- Maintenance teams planning and executing field work
- Stakeholders who need a live, visual overview of the network

How you’ll use it
1) Sign up and log in
   - Create an account (or log in with provided credentials).
   - Your session is kept secure using industry‑standard authentication.
2) Explore the dashboard
   - View overall health, alerts, and critical stats at a glance.
3) Plan work
   - Create and manage tasks (e.g., inspections, repairs), assign priorities and due dates.
4) Receive alerts
   - Get notified about faults or important updates.
5) Manage your profile
   - Update your account settings and security preferences.

Key screens
- Dashboard: Your operations home screen.
- Map & status views: Visualize grid assets and their status.
- Tasks: Create, update, and track maintenance tasks.
- Settings: Manage preferences (notifications, security), and account details.

Data and privacy
- Your account data and tasks are private to you and your team.
- Sessions use secure, modern web standards. Tokens are protected and requests are encrypted via HTTPS.

Support
- Encounter an issue? Reach out to the maintainer or open a ticket on the project’s repository.

That’s it! Vahini is built to be simple to navigate while still powerful for grid operations.
